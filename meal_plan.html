Camping Meal Plan (Nov 8-10)

A simple, hearty, and warm menu designed for ease of cooking in cool weather.

Saturday

Dinner: Foil Packet Sausage & Veggies

This is quick to cook and has minimal cleanup, perfect for your first night.

  • At home: Chop up bell peppers, onions, and small potatoes. Slice pre-cooked sausage (like chicken apple sausage or kielbasa).
  • At camp: Toss veggies and sausage with olive oil, salt, pepper, and spices in a large bowl. Divide into individual foil packets. Crimp the edges tightly and cook directly in the campfire embers for 20-30 minutes, flipping once.

Sunday

Breakfast: Classic Camp Breakfast

  • Bacon or breakfast sausage cooked in a cast-iron skillet.
  • Scrambled eggs.
  • Hot coffee, tea, or hot chocolate.

Lunch: No-Cook Wraps or Sandwiches

  • Tortillas or sturdy bread with deli meat, cheese, lettuce, and condiments.
  • Serve with chips, apples, and trail mix. Easy to eat while relaxing or on a short hike.

Dinner: Campfire Chili & Cornbread

  • At home: Cook a batch of your favorite chili and store it in a sealed container.
  • At camp: Simply reheat the chili in a pot over the fire or on your camp stove. You can cook cornbread in a cast iron skillet or dutch oven over the fire, or bring pre-made muffins to warm up. Top with cheese and sour cream.

Monday

Breakfast: Quick Oatmeal & Coffee

  • Instant oatmeal packets are perfect for a quick, warm breakfast on the morning you're packing up.
  • Finish any leftover snacks.
