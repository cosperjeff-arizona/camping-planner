Camping Meal Plan (Nov 8-10)

A streamlined menu designed for minimal prep and easy campfire cooking. All Friday prep work helps make camp cooking effortless.

Saturday

Breakfast: Grab-and-Go

  • Hostess donuts or pastries
  • Fresh fruit (apples, clementines)
  • Instant coffee or tea

Dinner: Steak with Foil Packet Potatoes & Onions

A hearty first-night meal cooked directly on the campfire grate and in the coals.

  • Shopping: 1 steak (ribeye, strip, or favorite cut ~8 oz), beef seasoning, 1 small Yukon gold potato, ¼–½ onion, olive oil. Optional: butter, fresh herbs.
  • Friday prep: Slice potato and onion thin. Toss with olive oil and beef seasoning, seal in foil packet. Season steak lightly or wait until camp. Chill in fridge.
  • At camp: Place foil packet in hot coals for 20-25 minutes. Grill steak over fire grate to desired doneness. Let rest 5 minutes before serving.

Sunday

Breakfast: Easy Morning

  • Hostess donuts or pastries
  • Fresh fruit
  • Instant coffee or tea
  • Trail mix or granola bar

Lunch: Lunchable Snack Box (On the Trail)

Perfect for a hike around Stoneman Lake or relaxing at camp.

  • Shopping: Crackers (Triscuits, Ritz, Wheat Thins), 2 oz cheddar or gouda, 4-6 slices summer sausage/salami/pepperoni, 1 apple or small bunch of grapes, 1 granola bar. Optional: mustard packet or hot honey.
  • Friday prep: Slice cheese, portion crackers, cheese, meat, and fruit into a container. Toss apple slices with lemon juice if prepping ahead. Add granola bar and mustard packet. Store cheese and meat in cooler until Sunday morning, then pack in daypack.
  • For the hike: Pack in daypack with a frozen water bottle or ice pack to keep cool.

Dinner: Cajun Chicken & Rice (Foil Packet)

All-in-one foil packet meal that's flavorful and filling.

  • Shopping: 2 boneless, skinless chicken thighs, ½ cup white rice (dry), ¼ onion, ¼ bell pepper (optional), Cajun seasoning, garlic powder, butter or oil.
  • Friday prep: Cook ½ cup white rice until ~90% done, then cool. Dice onion and bell pepper. Assemble foil packet with rice on bottom, 2 chicken thighs on top, veggies scattered over, Cajun seasoning, garlic powder, salt, pepper, and 1 tbsp butter. Seal tightly and chill.
  • At camp: Place packet in hot coals or on grate for 25-30 minutes, flipping halfway. Check that chicken reaches 165°F internally.

Monday

Breakfast: Quick Pack-Up Morning

  • Leftover pastries or snacks
  • Trail mix, beef jerky
  • Fresh fruit
  • Instant coffee or tea

Snacks Throughout Trip

  • Trail mix (pre-portioned)
  • Beef jerky or snack sticks
  • Extra crackers or granola bars
  • Fresh fruit (apples, clementines)

Packing Guide

Cooler

Steak, potato & onion foil packet, Cajun chicken & rice foil packet, cheese & meat for snack box, fruit, butter, condiments.

Dry Goods Tote

Crackers, spices, instant coffee/tea, trail mix, beef jerky, pastries, granola bars, fruit (non-refrigerated), cooking gear (grate, tongs, spatula, foil, lighter).

Sunday Daypack

Lunchable snack box, napkin, water bottle/hydration bladder, optional ice pack or frozen water bottle.
